Ebitengine Holiday Hack Jam 2023 Begins

Hajime Hoshi
December 22, 2023

Organized by kettek, a heavy user of Ebitengine, the game jam Ebitengine Holiday Hack 2023 will be held. The event will take place over three weeks during the year-end and New Year holidays. If you want to create a game with Ebitengine during this time, please join us!
